About Us - Mythic Rapids

Welcome to Mythic Rapids, your premier destination for thrilling whitewater rafting adventures.

At Mythic Rapids, we are passionate about providing our clients with unforgettable experiences on the water. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, our expert guides will ensure your safety while delivering an exhilarating adventure.

History

Founded in 1995, Mythic Rapids has been providing unforgettable rafting experiences for over 25 years. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety while delivering an exhilarating adventure on the water.
